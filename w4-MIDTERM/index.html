Jurassic Park III

Jurassic Park III follows Dr. Alan Grant as he was persuaded by a "wealthy" businessman to conduct a tour of Isla Sorna, InGen's secondary site for failed experiments, but after landing on the island finds to find the man's son. You travel avoiding Velociraptors, and an abnormally large Spinosaurus.

Company Credits: Universal Pictures, Amblin Entertainment

Release Date: July 18, 2001

Genres: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi

Rating: PG-13

Running Time: 92 minutes
