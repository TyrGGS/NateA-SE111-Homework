REVIEW

Nathan Asstafan

April 18, 2026

Jurassic Park III is a fun and action-packed sequel that keeps the danger high from beginning to end. I understand not many people enjoy this movie because there is no real development within the movie, the characters and just the story itself did not really drive that home, but it makes up with action from a new antagonist, the Spinosaurus, that follows them through the entire movie.

I think Jurassic Park III is entertaining. It has suspense, strong dinosaur scenes, and a survival feel that makes it exciting to watch. It is a good movie for fans who want more action and less waiting around.
