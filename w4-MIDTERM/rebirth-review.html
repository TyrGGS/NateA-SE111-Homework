REVIEW

Nathan Asstafan

April 18, 2026

Jurassic World: Rebirth is in ways very similar to JP3, with mercenaries and others to help the journey travel to a different site where failed experiments of dinosaurs roam the island. Once again, many people don't like that there isn't any development, and the story seemingly didn't add up to them. I enjoyed the movie because of the action it brought to the table. In a world of dinosaurs, I don't necessarily see a point in developing these characters too much.

What interests me most about Jurassic World: Rebirth is that it seems like it could expand the series in a different direction while still respecting what fans enjoy. The movie already looks intense, visually strong, and full of suspense, which is why I am excited to see how it turns out.

Trivia

  • Early on Jonathan Bailey is wearing the same colour combo Dr. Grant wore in the first Jurassic film, blue button up shirt and khakis. He later makes reference to working under Dr. Grant.
  • Takes place five years after the events of Jurassic World: Dominion (2022).
  • A deleted scene reportedly showed a Mutadon attempting to mimic human speech patterns, a nod to the Velociraptor intelligence seen in Jurassic Park III, though this was cut for pacing reasons.
